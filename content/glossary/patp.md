# @p

A **`@p`** (pronounced "pat P") is an [atom](atom) [aura](aura) in [Hoon](hoon). This aura is used for ship names like `~sampel-palnet`, `~pocwet`, `~zod`, etc. It is often used informally to refer to an Urbit ID.
